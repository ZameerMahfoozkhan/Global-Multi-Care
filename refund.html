Refund Policy

At Global Multi Care Solution, we aim to provide reliable and satisfactory repair services. This Refund Policy outlines the terms under which refunds may be considered.

1. Service Charges

Service or inspection charges are non-refundable once the technician has visited and the inspection has been completed.

2. Eligibility for Refund

A refund may be considered in the following cases:

  • If a payment is made but the service is not delivered
  • If there is a duplicate payment made by the customer
  • If the service request is canceled before the technician is assigned or dispatched

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • After successful completion of the service
  • If the issue reoccurs due to external factors or misuse
  • If the customer denies or delays the service after technician dispatch
  • For spare parts once installed

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, customers must contact us with their booking details. Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days.

5. Mode of Refund

Refunds will be issued through the original mode of payment or any other method as agreed between both parties.
